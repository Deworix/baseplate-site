Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

The short version: this website sets no cookies and uses no advertising or cross-site tracking.

1. Cookies we use

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, local storage, fingerprinting, or cross-site tracking. The static site sends a small fixed set of identifier-free aggregate funnel events, without an account, device or session ID or free-form content, and respects Global Privacy Control and Do Not Track. Because no information is stored on your device for this measurement, there are no non-essential cookies to accept.

2. Third-party requests

The site uses local system fonts and makes no third-party font request. If you buy a plan, you are taken to our payment provider's own checkout, which operates under its own cookie and privacy policies.

3. The Baseplate app

The desktop application is not a website and does not use cookies. It stores its settings locally on your own computer.

4. If this changes

If we ever add analytics or other non-essential cookies, we will update this policy and show a proper consent banner that lets you accept or decline before any such cookie is set.

5. Contact

Questions? Email contact@baseplatedev.com, or use our contact form. See also our Privacy Policy.
