Cookie Policy

Last updated: [DD MONTH 2026]

The short version: this website sets no cookies and uses no tracking.

1. Cookies we use

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any cross-site tracking. The baseplate.run marketing site is a static site and does not store cookies on your device. Because we set no non-essential cookies, no cookie-consent banner is required.

2. Third-party requests

To display the site we load web fonts from Google Fonts. This does not set cookies, but Google receives your IP address as part of serving the font files. If you buy a plan, you are taken to our payment provider's own checkout, which operates under its own cookie and privacy policies.

3. The Baseplate app

The desktop application is not a website and does not use cookies. It stores its settings locally on your own computer.

4. If this changes

If we ever add analytics or other non-essential cookies, we will update this policy and show a proper consent banner that lets you accept or decline before any such cookie is set.

5. Contact

Questions? Email us via the contact form. See also our Privacy Policy.
